Apollo 11 Moon Landing

On July 20, 1969, humanity took its first steps on the moon. This is the story of Apollo 11.

The Launch

The Saturn V rocket launched from Kennedy Space Center on July 16, 1969.

Journey to the Moon

The spacecraft traveled 240,000 miles to reach the moon.

Landing on the Moon

The lunar module, Eagle, landed on the moon's surface.

First Steps

Neil Armstrong said, "That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind."

Return to Earth

The crew safely returned to Earth on July 24, 1969.
